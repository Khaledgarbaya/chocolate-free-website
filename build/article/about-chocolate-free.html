About Chocolate Free

Chocolate free is a culinary diary of a chocoholic, sweet tooth young lady trying to re-create new sweet fruity and chocolaty version of some classic, or not, deserts.

In this blog you will find dessert recipes, tips and twists from the simple pancake to an authentic Parisian sophisticated desert .I might go out of the main subject sometimes and suggest weekly brunch / breakfasts inspiration ideas in the “Hors sujet ” section you should have a look there too.

So welcome everyone, fasten your apron belt and enjoy the ride on this sweet roller coaster in Yumminess-wonderland.
